Spatiotemporal variability during dual-task TUG increases progressively from healthy young adults to healthy older adults to early Parkinson's disease.

Evidence:
[[Coefficient of variation]] was used to quantify variability across groups. Early PD showed the highest variability in both spatiotemporal and pressure measures relative to both healthy control groups.
